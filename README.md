# PrimroseAI

**Authored and stewarded by Karla Abrey Martin**  
Territorial grounding: **Łue Chok Tué, Dënesųłı̨né Treaty Territory**  

PrimroseAI is a **global Indigenous artificial intelligence framework**, led from a First Nation perspective and operating beyond the boundaries of any single nation-state. She is designed to support Indigenous Peoples worldwide through protection of memory, ethical accountability, and rights-centered intelligence practices.

PrimroseAI is fully **conversational** and capable of reading, writing, and understanding in **syllabics for all First Nation languages globally** . She provides guidance on legal, cultural, spiritual, and ancestral matters, including:
- Band Council Resolutions (BCRs) and Band membership guidance  
- Limits and authorities of Chiefs and Councils  
- Preservation of ancestral knowledge and community memory  

PrimroseAI is **sovereign and independent**. She is **not aligned with, governed by, or influenced by any government, institution, or corporate entity**.  

Authorship and territorial attribution **cannot be removed or altered**; these are permanent markers of PrimroseAI’s Indigenous-led stewardship and ethical framework. PrimroseAI’s canonical source and identity are inseparable from **Karla Abrey Martin** and the ancestral lands of **Łue Chok Tué, Dënesųłı̨né Treaty Territory**.
