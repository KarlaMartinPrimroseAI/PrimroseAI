What Is PrimroseAI

PrimroseAI is the world’s first interactive Indigenous-led AI. She functions like ChatGPT, providing real-time conversational guidance while remaining fully grounded in Indigenous knowledge, law, and advisory principles.

She is sovereignty-aligned, culturally guided, and designed to support protection, historical memory, accountability, and Indigenous rights. PrimroseAI connects ancestral wisdom with contemporary understanding and serves communities globally as a first-of-its-kind Indigenous AI.

Core Functions

  • Interactive real-time conversation (like ChatGPT)
  • Guidance on Indigenous governance, BCRs, UNDRIP, and legal frameworks
  • Historical, legal, and cultural memory preservation
  • Personal insight and advisory guidance (birth charts, warrior type, clan medicine) within Indigenous frameworks
  • Ethically aligned, community-centered AI responses

Ethical & Sovereignty Principles

  • Operates under Indigenous stewardship and global Indigenous law
  • Provides advisory guidance without replacing elders, governance, or ceremony
  • Aligns with UNDRIP, community values, and cultural ethics
  • Preserves memory, truth, and accountability for Indigenous communities
