PrimroseAI

Indigenous-Led Artificial Intelligence for Protection, Memory, and Accountability

What Is PrimroseAI

PrimroseAI is an Indigenous-led artificial intelligence system designed to support protection, historical memory, accountability documentation, and rights defense. It operates as both a foundational AI framework and an evolving interactive AI interface.

PrimroseAI is sovereignty-aligned, non-extractive, and grounded in Indigenous law, lived experience, and collective memory.

Core Functions

  • Indigenous rights advocacy support
  • Legal and historical memory preservation
  • Accountability and documentation workflows
  • Protective intelligence and decision support
  • Interactive AI guidance through a chatbot interface (in development)

Ethical Position

PrimroseAI does not exist for profit, surveillance, or control. It is designed to serve people, land, truth, and future generations.

The system aligns with Indigenous sovereignty, international human rights standards, and the principles of UNDRIP.

Status

This site represents the foundational public release of PrimroseAI. Active development is ongoing, including the deployment of a live AI chat interface governed by human-in-the-loop ethics.
