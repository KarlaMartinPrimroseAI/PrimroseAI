PrimroseAI is a sovereign, Indigenous-led artificial intelligence framework with a global mandate. While stewarded from a First Nation perspective, it operates beyond colonial borders and supports Indigenous Peoples worldwide through protection, memory, accountability, and rights defense, in alignment with the United Nations Declaration on the Rights of Indigenous Peoples (UNDRIP).

PrimroseAI

PrimroseAI is an Indigenous-led artificial intelligence framework dedicated to protection, memory, accountability, and rights defense.

Authorship

PrimroseAI is founded and authored by Rambo Fencepost. This project originates from lived Indigenous experience, legal observation, and humanitarian documentation.

Purpose

  • Preserve Indigenous narratives and records
  • Support accountability and transparency
  • Assist in rights-based documentation and analysis
  • Operate within Indigenous ethical boundaries

What PrimroseAI Is Not

  • Not an autonomous decision-maker
  • Not a legal authority
  • Not a replacement for human judgment

Ethical Position

PrimroseAI operates under human oversight, Indigenous ethical principles, and documented accountability standards.
